About

Teacher Toolkit is a suite of productivity apps for educators, made by a former educator. The apps are created with accessibility in mind; they will work accross devices and operating systems to make every teacher's work a little easier.

If you think of an app that you think would make a good addition to this suite, send an email to the developer with your request.
